# Business & ESG Performance Overview Dashboard

This Tableau dashboard project offers an interactive overview of critical business performance metrics, as well as Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) indicators, across a variety of industries and regions from 2015 to 2025.


## Key Features

- **Growth Rate Analysis**: Trend line showing annual growth rates and breakdowns by industry.
- **Financial KPIs**: Displays average revenue, market capitalization, and profit margin.
- **ESG Insights**: Visualizes ESG Overall Scores by year and industry, highlighting sustainability performance.
- **Correlation Analysis**: Scatter plot examining the relationship between ESG scores and business growth.
- **Filter Interactivity**: Allows filtering by year, region, and industry for targeted analysis.


## Tools Used

- **Tableau**: For data visualization and dashboard creation.
- **SQL (PostgreSQL)**: For data cleaning, transformation, and analysis.
- **Excel**: For initial data exploration and formatting.

## Insights

- A **positive correlation** is observed between ESG scores and business growth rates.
- **Technology** and **Healthcare** industries outperform their counterparts.
- **Consistent improvement** in ESG scores year over year, suggesting that there is a growing awareness of sustainability.
